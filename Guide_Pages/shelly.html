Shelly is a defensive brawlers which mainly focuses around protecting her allies from melee brawlers.
📖Overview

Shelly is a Damage Dealer brawler who thrives as Anti-Tank. Being a starting brawler she doesn't have any outstanding feature's, which sets her apart from other brawlers.

Shelly playstyle is about getting close to enemies, and excel on: Brawl Ball, Gem Gram and Showdown. Her main weakness is being outranged or ways to avoid her super.
